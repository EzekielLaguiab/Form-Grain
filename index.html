Our Story

Craft is a Dialogue
Between Wood & Hand

Founded in 2008 in the forests of Vermont, Form & Grain grew from a single workshop and an obsession with honest materials. Every joinery is hand-cut. Every finish is oil-rubbed. Every piece ships with a hand-written provenance card.

  • FSC-certified hardwoods from regenerative forests
  • No particle board, no veneers — solid wood only
  • In-house craftspeople, never outsourced
  • Carbon-neutral shipping on all US orders
